About Us

Company Background

Our company is aspiring to be a pioneering actor uniquely planting a new seed of change and illuminating hope in the future of the Ethiopia’s tourism industry. It was established in December 2021 by a group of innovative Developers who determined to make a difference in the service Industry. The objectives behind its establishment are to provide a World class service in the sector, explore and market exotic attractions obscured because of less visibility and ultimately justify the renaissance of Ethiopia’s tourism sector to the global community

Vision

To be among the 5 leading companies in the hospitality industry of eastern Africa in 2025.

Mission

Offer world class services in tour operation, Car rent, Event management, travel arrangement and guidance enriching with innovative ideas at a competitive price.
